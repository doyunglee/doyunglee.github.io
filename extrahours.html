Overview

An Olin College alumnus wanted a mobile app that would help him keep track of how much time he spent on his daily tasks. I designed the interface of the app that would allow him to create schedules for each day of the week and keep track of how long his tasks take.

Left: Greeting screen for app. Right: To-do schedule for Tuesdays.


Timer interaction for app. Press 'start' to begin timing your task. Once you are done, you can hit the green checkmark to move onto the next task.
